Common Surgical Problems

  • Blood Clots
  • Scarring

Common Remedies

  • Follow post-operative care instructions carefully.
  • Take prescribed medications on time.
  • Keep the surgical site clean and dry.
  • Attend all follow-up appointments with your surgeon.
  • Maintain a healthy diet to promote healing.

Prevention Tips

  • Choose a qualified and experienced surgeon.
  • Discuss all risks and complications before surgery.
  • Quit smoking and avoid alcohol before surgery.
  • Follow pre-surgery guidelines provided by your doctor.
  • Stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle.
