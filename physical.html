Physical Health Issues

Back Pain

Causes: Poor posture, lack of exercise, muscle strain.

Remedies: Stretching, physical therapy, pain relievers.

Preventions: Maintain good posture, exercise regularly, avoid heavy lifting.

Obesity

Causes: Overeating, lack of physical activity, genetics.

Remedies: Balanced diet, regular exercise, medical consultation.

Preventions: Healthy eating habits, active lifestyle, portion control.

Diabetes

Causes: High sugar intake, obesity, family history.

Remedies: Medication, insulin therapy, diet control.

Preventions: Monitor blood sugar, healthy diet, regular exercise.

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Causes: High salt intake, stress, lack of exercise.

Remedies: Medication, reduced salt intake, stress management.

Preventions: Regular exercise, healthy diet, limit alcohol consumption.

Arthritis

Causes: Aging, joint injury, autoimmune disorders.

Remedies: Pain relievers, physical therapy, joint surgery.

Preventions: Maintain a healthy weight, exercise regularly, protect joints.

Asthma

Causes: Allergies, air pollution, respiratory infections.

Remedies: Inhalers, avoiding triggers, medication.

Preventions: Avoid allergens, quit smoking, regular check-ups.

Migraine

Causes: Stress, hormonal changes, certain foods.

Remedies: Painkillers, rest, avoiding triggers.

Preventions: Manage stress, regular sleep, stay hydrated.

Heart Disease

Causes: High cholesterol, smoking, sedentary lifestyle.

Remedies: Medication, surgery, lifestyle changes.

Preventions: Healthy diet, regular exercise, avoid smoking.

Osteoporosis

Causes: Aging, calcium deficiency, lack of vitamin D.

Remedies: Calcium supplements, medication, weight-bearing exercises.

Preventions: Adequate calcium intake, regular exercise, avoid smoking.

Insomnia

Causes: Stress, anxiety, poor sleep habits.

Remedies: Sleep hygiene, relaxation techniques, medication.

Preventions: Regular sleep schedule, avoid caffeine, reduce screen time.
