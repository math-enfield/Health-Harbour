Famous Online Nutrition Tracking Apps

Here are some popular apps to help you track your nutrition and maintain a healthy lifestyle:

MyFitnessPal

Description: Track your diet and exercise with a large food database.

Website: www.myfitnesspal.com

Cronometer

Description: Track calories, macros, and micronutrients with precision.

Website: www.cronometer.com

Lose It!

Description: A simple app for calorie counting and weight loss.

Website: www.loseit.com

FatSecret

Description: Track your diet, exercise, and weight with a supportive community.

Website: www.fatsecret.com
