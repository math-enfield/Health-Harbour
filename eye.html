Common Eye Problems

  • Myopia (Nearsightedness)
  • Hyperopia (Farsightedness)
  • Astigmatism
  • Dry Eye Syndrome
  • Cataracts

Common Remedies

  • Wear prescription glasses or contact lenses.
  • Use lubricating eye drops for dry eyes.
  • Follow the 20-20-20 rule: Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
  • Eat a diet rich in vitamins A, C, and E.
  • Visit your eye doctor regularly for check-ups.
